Prose: prediction of super-enhancers

Super-enhancers are cluster of enhancers which play a key role in cell type specific gene expression and have been found highly associated with disease like cancer. Since super-enhancers are less in number and cell type specific, it will be useful to discover and characterize super-enhancers rather than exploring thousands of enhancers operating in a cell. However, identification is challenging at this stage due to limited knowledge of potential features and master transcription factors for many cell types. Here, we developed a novel machine learning based method Prose to predict super-enhancers.
